About Me

Hello! My name is Corinne Piorkowski, and I am a journalism junior at UT-Austin. I aspire to be a news reporter in any medium post-graduating! I love human interest and feature writing, and after a summer internship in the KUT newsroom, I now love recording news spots and radio as a whole. Born and raised in Austin, I also love reporting on local Austin news and events. I am passionate about keeping the Austin public informed on current events and highlighting stories that matter most to people who live here. Thank you, and feel free to contact me through the form below!
